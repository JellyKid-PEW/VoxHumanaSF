What this is

Vox Humana is a story about what happens after the noise stops— when survival becomes routine, and connection becomes the harder work.

Character-led

Relationships are the engine. Tension lives in proximity, trust is earned slowly, and the crew forms by accumulation rather than declaration.

Fringe survival

Broken infrastructure, quiet debts, small jobs, and a ship kept alive through habit, maintenance, and refusal to quit.

Serialized by design

Twice-monthly core chapters. Optional interstitial releases land between drops— additive, not a replacement for story progress.

Before you begin

Vox Humana starts quiet on purpose. This isn’t an excerpt from Chapter One—just a short tone note so you know what kind of story you’re stepping into: pressure in the small moments, meaning in the pauses, and momentum that builds by accumulation.
